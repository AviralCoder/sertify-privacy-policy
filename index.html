Privacy Policy of Sertify - Search Countries App

Throughout this document, the terms “we”, “us”, “our” & “ours” refer to me as the developer of this app. And the terms “you”, “your” “yours” & refer to YOU (as the user of this app).

Personal Data

‘Personal Data’ means any information that may be used to identify an individual, directly or indirectly, in particular by reference to an identifier, including not limited to, a first and last name, physical address, email address, online identifier or other contact information and/or identity proof documents.

What personal data do we collect?

There is no user personal data collected in this app. However, we do access these device properties -

  • The Width of your device.
  • Your status bar height.
  • Your Clipboard

Where do we obtain your Personal Data from?

The Width of the Device and the Status Bar Height are accessed when you open the app itself.

The Clipboard is accessed when you click on one of the country cards OR when you click the flag url.

How do we use your personal information?

We use the personal information for user experience. The width of the device and the status bar height have been accessed only for the app layout.

Your clipboard is accessed by the app to set your clipboard string to a certain info. (Please check the Where do we obtain your Personal Data from? to see how your clipbaord is accessed)


WE NEVER GET THE STRING FOR YOUR CLIPBOARD, WE ONLY SET IT.

How long do we access your personal data?

Your personal data(precisely your device width and status bar height) is accessed only when you have the app open or opened in the background.

Your clipboard is accessed when you click either on a country card or the link inside it for setting information.

Links to other Websites

Our app does contain links but on clicking those links, it won't lead to the website. On clicking those links, the link will be copied on your clipboard and it is asked for YOU to paste it in your browser. The links are totally safe and lead to the flag of a country.
